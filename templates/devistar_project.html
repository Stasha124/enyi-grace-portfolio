üè¢ Project Overview

This project involved designing and implementing a role-based business information system in Microsoft Access for Devistar Wire Company, a manufacturing and distribution firm in Nigeria. The system centralizes operations such as copper sourcing, coating imports, semi-finished production, inventory management, and branch-level sales reporting.

Previously, the company relied on spreadsheets that caused inconsistencies and inefficiencies. This new system introduced secure authentication, user-specific dashboards, and automated reports for customers, employees, and managers.

üí° System Features & Design

  1. Role-Based Access: Login authentication (via LoginTbl) assigns access to Customers, Employees, or Managers with DLookup-based redirection.
  2. Database Architecture: UML Entity-Relationship Diagram (ERD) linking all core tables with referential integrity across customers, products, and orders.
  3. Operational Workflow: Tracks copper sourcing, coating imports, production batches, and finished goods sales per branch.
  4. User Interfaces: Role-specific dashboards for customers, employees, and managers with dynamic form visibility and real-time data validation.
  5. Automation: VBA scripts automate validation, conditional formatting, and live calculations (e.g., low stock alerts).
  6. Reporting: Parameterized queries and printable reports filtered by branch, customer, or date ‚Äî supporting informed decisions.
